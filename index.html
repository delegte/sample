How to make a cup of tea

  1. Treat your water kindly.
  2. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up.
  3. Add tea and water.
  4. Pop a tea bag into your mug, pour over the hot water and stir briefly.
  5. Wait patiently.
  6. Give it a squeeze.
  7. Customise your brew.
