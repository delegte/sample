How to make a cup of tea

Treat your water kindly. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Add tea and water. Pop a tea bag into your mug, pour over the hot water and stir briefly. Wait patiently. Give it a squeeze. Customise your brew.
